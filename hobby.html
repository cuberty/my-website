My Hobbies

Introduction to Hobbies

Hobbies are activities that allow me to express my creativity, unwind, and continuously learn. They're essential to my well-being, offering a balance to my busy professional and personal life. Whether I'm solving a puzzle, traveling to new places, or enjoying a good game, hobbies enrich my life with new experiences and personal growth.

Specific Hobbies

Gaming

I’ve always had a passion for video games, from retro consoles to modern platforms. Gaming is not only a form of entertainment but also a way to connect with friends and immerse myself in different worlds. I enjoy a variety of gaming genres, including action, strategy, and RPGs. Some of my favorite consoles include the PS5, Xbox Series S, Nintendo Switch, and older handheld systems like the Nintendo 3DS and PS Vita.

Speedcubing

Another passion of mine is speedcubing. I love solving the Rubik's Cube and other puzzles competitively. It's a great way to challenge my problem-solving skills and improve my focus. I’ve participated in World Cube Association (WCA) events and enjoy collecting different types of puzzles.

Traveling

Exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, and trying new foods are some of my favorite things to do. My most memorable trip was to Japan, where I explored both the bustling cities and serene countryside. Traveling broadens my perspective and fills me with inspiration.

Learning New Skills

I am always on the lookout for new skills to learn, whether it's programming, languages, or anything that sparks my curiosity. Currently, I’m diving into learning Python, VBA, and SQL. It’s a fulfilling way to challenge myself and expand my knowledge, which is essential in both my personal and professional life.

Highlights

The hobby that influences me the most is speedcubing. The concentration and problem-solving skills required to solve puzzles like the Rubik’s Cube help me in many areas of my life, from my work to personal growth. I also find that gaming provides me with the opportunity to relax and take a break, while learning new programming languages keeps my mind engaged and continuously improving.

Traveling is another huge part of my life. It allows me to step out of my comfort zone, connect with different cultures, and come back with a fresh perspective on life. Every trip I take adds a new chapter to my journey and inspires my projects and goals.
